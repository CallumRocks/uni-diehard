ABOUT

Written by Jeb Stuart and Steven E. de Souza and directed by John McTiernan.

The Movie

NYPD officer John McClane must fight a group of well equipped terrorists led by Hans Gruber who have seized the Nakatomi Plaza on Christmas Eve to rob a corporate vault containing million of dollars in bearer bonds. McClane must make sure his wife Holly Gennero and the other hostages stay safe while helping Sergeant Al Powell control the situation.

Nakatomi Plaza

Nakatomi Plaza is a high-rise office building in Century City, Los Angeles, California that houses the headquarters of the California branch of the Nakatomi Corporation, a Japanese company.

Characters

The movies main leads are John McClane the protaganist, Hans Gruber the antagonist, Al Powell the sidekick, Holly Gennero a hostage and Johns wife. Also featured in the film are the terrorists, Joseph the CEO of Nakatomi Industries and Harry the hero.

CHARACTERS

John
McClane

John McClane is an American detective lieutenant with the New York City Police Department. John has worked in the force since 1977. He is separated from his wife, Holly Gennero McClane who moved to Los Angeles several months ago to pursue a career that John ultimately thought would fail which lead to their separation. They have two children, Lucy and John Jr.
